This page contains educational video resources about preparing for technical interviews. 

1) This video shares a step-by-step method to approach technical interviews involving live coding. The tips are important because the biggest thing people struggle with is being able to think of algorithm designs on the spot while also explaining your thinking.  Key Points: Best coding practices, how to explain your algorithm, designing algorithms, how to practice for technical interviews

2) This video shares good examples of behavioral questions and some sample ways to respond to those questions. This content is important because being able to present your character and personality well is a big part of landing a job. Key Points: Behavioral interview questions, formatting anecdotes, organizing answers using PAR method
