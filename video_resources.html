Video Resources

Many software experts have created great video tutorials about preparing for technical interviews. Here, we've compiled some of our favorite video resources, highlighted the key points, and referenced the creators so that you can go learn more from them!

1) This video shares a step-by-step method to approach technical interviews involving live coding. The tips are important because the biggest thing people struggle with is being able to think of algorithm designs on the spot while also explaining your thinking.  

Key Points: Best coding practices, how to explain your algorithm, designing algorithms, how to practice for technical interviews

 Source: Engineering With Utsav (YouTube) 

2) This video walks through a classic technical interview problem called FizzBuzz. Through humor and straightforward explanations, the creator explains the thought process and creation of an algorithm to solve this problem on the spot. Walk through the video in real time to get some good practice solving a standard interview problem. 

Key Points: Problem walkthrough, algorithm design, explaining logic, efficient programming on a time crunch

 Source: Fireship (YouTube) 

3) This video provides an excellent roadmap for topics to study when preparing for technical interviews. The creator walks through a chart that connects different data structures, and explains why studying in a certain order is optimal for understanding data structures.

Key Points: Optimal study plans, understanding data structures, connecting Computer Science concepts

 Source: NeetCode (YouTube) 

4) This video is a full-length, mock technical interview, and it is a helpful resource to simulate the actual interviewing experience. The interviewee sees a problem for the first time and walks through the steps of designing and implementing a solution. Follow along with this video in order to understand the environment you'll be in when doing your own interview!

Key Points: Mock interview, interview environment and conditions, interview experience, standard problem, JavaScript

 Source: Tech With Nader (YouTube) 

5) This video is a short and entertaining guide to succeeding at technical interviews. The creator shares 10 great tips for excelling at technical interviews, including how to pick your programming language, and make other important decisions while you're on the spot. The creator also discusses tips for how to present your best self, beyond just your coding skills, during your interview.

Key Points: Best coding practices, deciding how to approach the problem, discussing solutions, presenting yourself well

 Source: TechLead (YouTube) 

6) This video discusses a scary but common experience -- freezing up during an interview. While no one wants to be in this position, it's important to prepare for that possibility. This video provides a lot of helpful tips and strategies of what to do when you are unable to proceed with the problem during an interview. In a concise format, the creator shares some very valuable information that can help get you back on track during a stressful situation.

Key Points: Problem solving, decision-making during an interview, navigating unexpected and stressful situations
