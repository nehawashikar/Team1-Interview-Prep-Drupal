Video Resources 

Many software experts have created great video tutorials about preparing for technical interviews. Here, we've compiled some of our favorite video resources, highlighted the key points, and referenced the creators so that you can go learn more from them!

Quick Tips 

This section is for you if you are short on time or wants some general tips on presenting yourself and your answers in an interview. All the videos in this section are shorter in length, and are primarily discussion style. You won't find too much coding here.

Problem Walkthroughs 

These videos walk through several classic interview problems and introduce the logic and process you should use when you are faced with a new coding problem on-the-spot. These resources are medium length (~10-20 minutes), and are essential resources if you are still learning how to approach different styles of coding problems.

Mock Technical Interviews 

The resources in this section are long-form mock technical interviews. These are great videos to follow along with to simulate the interview environment, and help you understand what to expect when you are in your interview. The videos go through some very common interview style questions, so it is a great idea to follow them as if you are the interviewee.
