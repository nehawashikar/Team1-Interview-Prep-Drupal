Video Resources - Quick Tips 

The videos on this page are shorter-form, discussion videos that share some quick tips on planning your preparation, presenting yourself well at interviews, and best practices to succeed during interviews.

This video provides an excellent roadmap for topics to study when preparing for technical interviews. The creator walks through a chart that connects different data structures, and explains why studying in a certain order is optimal for understanding data structures.

Key Points: Optimal study plans, understanding data structures, connecting Computer Science concepts

 Source: NeetCode (YouTube) 

This video is a short and entertaining guide to succeeding at technical interviews. The creator shares 10 great tips for excelling at technical interviews, including how to pick your programming language, and make other important decisions while you're on the spot. The creator also discusses tips for how to present your best self, beyond just your coding skills, during your interview.

Key Points: Best coding practices, deciding how to approach the problem, discussing solutions, presenting yourself well

 Source: TechLead (YouTube) 

This video discusses a scary but common experience -- freezing up during an interview. While no one wants to be in this position, it's important to prepare for that possibility. This video provides a lot of helpful tips and strategies of what to do when you are unable to proceed with the problem during an interview. In a concise format, the creator shares some very valuable information that can help get you back on track during a stressful situation.

Key Points: Problem solving, decision-making during an interview, navigating unexpected and stressful situations
