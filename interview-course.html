Interview Course 

Resume Building

Resumes allow job seekers to highlight any important skills or experiences they might have for any employers. A strong, well-written resume is the key to getting noticed by employers and landing a job. This module will go over how to write a strong resume. There are also additional resources and a quiz to demonstrate your understanding of this module. 

Job Search and Networking

Learning how to efficiently search for jobs that fit your skillset and network are extremely important skills in landing a job. This module will go over how to find jobs that are a good fit for your skillset, and how to network with people at companies you want to apply to in order to maximize your chances of getting an interview.  There are also additional resources and a quiz to demonstrate your understanding of this module. 

Interview Strategies

Doing well on the technical job interview is the deciding factor on if you end up landing the job or not. This module will go over many tips on the technical job interview in order to feel prepared for your interview and maximize your chance of success. There are also additional resources and a quiz to demonstrate your understanding of this module. 

Salary Negotiation

A major part after landing your job is negotiating your salary. This module will go over how to properly negotiate your salary and make sure that you are getting compensated fairly for the work that you will be doing for the company. There are also additional resources and a quiz to demonstrate your understanding of this module.
